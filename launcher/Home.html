Originally known as the now cancelled 'Luigi's Fight for the Lost Island,' this project aims to revamp the story and structure of the adventure as a way to experience all of the completed content for the original game. This includes 13 completed levels, 4 incomplete levels, tons of scrapped content, and more!

Story

Description

After blasting off away from the island into outer space chasing after Wart, catastrophe strikes and Luigi suffers a violent crash landing on the planet of Saturna. When he awakes, all of his memory of the adventure up to that point has mysteriously faded away. Luckily, nearby lies a building known as The Memory Center. Could this place help to recover his memories?
